# Five Lessons From My First Year as Engineering Manager

The transition from IC to manager is one of the hardest in a tech career.

## Your Calendar Is Now Your Product

As a manager, your output is decisions and unblocked people.

## Silence Is Not Agreement

In 1-1s, the person who talks least often has the most to say.

## Context Over Control

Provide context so your team can make good decisions without you.

## Technical Credibility Matters

Stay close to the codebase.

## Celebrate Shipping

Take a moment to acknowledge what was delivered.
