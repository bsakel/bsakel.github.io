# Welcome: Six Years Later (Short Version)

![Commit history that restarted this project](/assets/images/blog-repo-commit-history.png)

Welcome to my blog.

This space is where I share anything that seems interesting to me: engineering lessons, AI workflows, architecture decisions, and practical experiments.

This first post is the short version of how the blog finally shipped.

## The 5-Step Story

1. I found `terminal.css` and thought, “Let’s build a blog with this.”
2. While getting ready to create a new GitHub repo, I discovered I already had one.
3. I gave AI the CSS and the repo, then spent 3-4 focused hours building and shaping the site.
4. At around the classic “80% done” point, I hit a blocker: my old `Statiq.Web` setup had a 10-post limit. It’s a paid tool, and I had forgotten that detail.
5. AI saved the day by helping me migrate to `Statiq.Framework` in about 10 minutes, and we reached the goal.

## Why This Matters to Me

This project sat unfinished for six years.

Then, in one focused session, it became real.

The technical work mattered, but the bigger lesson was momentum. AI helped me remove a blocker at exactly the moment when older projects usually stall again.
